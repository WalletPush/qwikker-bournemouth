# AI Placeholder Generation Prompt for Qwikker

## Context
Generate abstract, category-based placeholder images for unclaimed business listings. These should be **non-specific detail shots** that don't represent real venues, maintaining trust and avoiding misrepresentation.

## Design Requirements
- **Style:** Abstract, dark, moody, premium
- **Format:** 16:9 landscape
- **Colors:** Dark backgrounds with subtle category-appropriate accent colors
- **Content:** Close-up details, textures, bokeh, silhouettes (NO identifiable venues or people)
- **Mood:** Sophisticated, cinematic, hints at the category without being literal

## Output Specifications
- **Resolution:** 1600Ã—900 px (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **Format:** WebP (compress to 40-120KB per image)
- **Naming Convention:** `{category}-{variant}.v1.webp`
  - Example: `restaurant-01.v1.webp`, `cafe-05.v1.webp`
- **File Size Target:** 40-120KB per image
- **Color Space:** sRGB

## Post-Processing
1. Export as high-quality JPG/PNG from AI generator
2. Convert to WebP using: `cwebp -q 85 input.jpg -o output.v1.webp`
3. Verify file size is under 120KB
4. Place in `/public/placeholders/{category}/` folder

## Usage in App
These placeholders will be:
- Used for unclaimed business listings (imported via Google Places)
- Selected deterministically based on `hash(google_place_id) % 10`
- Displayed with "UNCLAIMED" badge overlay
- Replaced with real photos when business claims listing

## Quick Start for ChatGPT/DALL-E

**Step 1:** Copy the prompt for one category (e.g., Restaurant)

**Step 3:** Request: "Generate all 10 variants described above"
**Step 4:** Download, convert to WebP, and organize into folders
**Step 5:** Repeat for remaining 15 categories
